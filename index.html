  • About us

    We are a company that fights for a better world!

    Biodegradable Bioplastics will help reduce and substitute the usage of plastic in several items. Even if some companies has been working to reduce the usage of plastic and make biodegradable substitutes the truth is that still those options are dangerous for species, even to humans. We will explain what kind of bioplastic can be use and in which products in this project. It is important for all to know this Bioplastic is currently used in disposable items like packaging, containers, straws, bags, nets, fertilizer, flowerpots, etc.
